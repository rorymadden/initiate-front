We partner with associations to deliver exceptional events which place them at the forefront of their industry.

Benefits
Positive Strategic Leadership as a Key Driver for Success

Associations need to support their members and industry by informing them of the latest challenges, changes and trends in the industry, facilitating networking and advocating on behalf of members in industry matters. A high quality conference delivers on all of these needs.

Insight & Leadership

Leading an industry organisation is a tough challenge. Associations cannot rely on authority and power alone. Great leadership requires a profound ability to influence. A well developed conference demonstrates the associations understanding of challenges, ability to identify and gathers those influencers who are pushing the industry forward and provides a platform of social influence on behalf of the association.

Networking

Business requires sales and people buy from people, not companies. Your members are looking to develop new contacts in the industry and expand their business networks. A conference provides an unrivalled opportunity for your members to network. By facilitating these opportunities and enabling new deals within your industry your reputation as an invaluable resource to the industry will be enhanced.

Ongoing Communication

A quality conference communicates a constant flow of relevant, valuable, information to their attendees and prospects in both the lead up to, and after, the event. Whether it is through interviews with speakers, highlighting a case study, webinars, or post show presentations, on-going communication allows your members to interact with you on a regular basis ensuring remain relevant, and front of mind, with your members for the other 360 days of the year.

Approach
The Initiate Methodology

Producing a two day conference typically takes up to seven months with numerous different skillsets required at key times from concept to delivery. We know this isn’t an associations core area, so Initiate.Network take onboard all upfront cost and risk but still deliver revenue through a profit sharing arrangement. We do this because we know what it takes to produce a top quality and successful conference that provides long-term value to the audience and the association and we guarantee great results.

  • The Plan

    Discovering Needs and Objectives

    There are many, often parallel, needs of an association; from increasing the membership base, representing your industry or educating members on new regulations. Whatever the goals, we work with you to develop a comprehensive project plan, demonstrating timelines and deliverables aligned to the associations core mission.

  • The Partnership

    Our expertise and resources join yours

    You know how to run an association, we know how to run conferences. At Initiate.Network we understand that associations have limited budgets and need to be risk averse. We propose a partnership based around profit sharing. We cover the upfront production costs of the event and you provide key sector insights. We will work with you on the marketing approach to ensure that it reflects your brand and message.

  • Research

    Translating awareness into understanding

    The real success of a conference stems from the initial research. Following the scope of association and regulatory objectives, we perform primary and secondary research to understand market challenges and needs. We then explore solutions and identify those leaders and disruptors who are solving issues or innovating within the industry.

  • Production

    Delivering a world-class agenda

    We source and secure the most sought after leaders and innovators in the industry, which is vital to a high profile event and world-class agenda. During production we also engineer the experience for attendees, which ties into the association's business objectives - more talks vs. more breakouts, and personalised experiences for the attendees.

  • Event & Sponsorship

    Strategic partnering to drive growth

    We source those vendors who provide leading and innovative solutions to the key industry challenges. We develop a suite of sponsorship opportunities from exhibition stands, on-stage branding, website advertisements to c-level dinners. Our sponsorship team work with the vendors and audience to ensure aligned value for all parties.

  • Sales & Marketing

    Attract and convert by building relationships

    Our mission is to enhance the reputation of both the association and the conference through ‘inbound’ sales and marketing. Throughout the research and production phase, we develop relevant, dual-branded content which we distribute through the associations marketing channels including web, email and social and our team create an ongoing database of fresh engaged prospects. We invite and work with industry, national and international media to ensure the event receives maximum exposure.

  • Event Management

    Bringing the Goal to Life

    Our team work together to ensure seamless integration throughout the project lifecycle. We manage every detail across the event from venue selection and stage design, attendee registration, and AV through to sponsor management and private events. We ensure all conference objectives are delivered resulting in an unbeatable experience for all attendees.

  • On-going Communication

    Continue the momentum

    Our mission is to provide lasting benefit for our partner associations. We deliver post-event analysis including attendee demographics, member/non member ratio and more which can feed directly into your associations strategy for the coming year. We also produce a content marketing plan for follow-up communication including material and media releases which can be released after the event to ensure you capitalise on the publicity and stay front of mind with your members and the industry.
