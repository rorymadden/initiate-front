We partner with associations to deliver premium events to put them at the forefront of their industry.

Benefits
For the Association and its Members

Associations need to be at the forefront of their industries, informing their members of the latest challenges, changes and trends, facilitating networking and advocating on behalf of its members in industry matters. A high quality conference delivers on all of these needs.

Insight & Leadership

A well developed conference brings industry-leading speakers to discuss and overcome the major challenges and trends being faced by the industry today.

Your reputation in your industry will be enhanced through the understanding of the challenges facing the industry, recognition of the leaders pushing the industry forward and the delivery of relevant training sessions.

Networking

Business requires sales and people buy from people, not companies. Your members are looking to develop new contacts in the industry and expand their business networks. A conference provides an unrivalled opportunity for your members to network.

By facilitating networking opportunities and enabling new deals within your industry your reputation as an invaluable resource for your members will be enhanced.

Ongoing Communication

Producing an event results in the creation of industry relevant, topical content. Whether it is through interviews with speakers, insights into a case study, webinars, or post show presentations, there is an abundance of both pre and post event content which can be communicated to your members.

On-going communication helps you remain relevant, and front of mind, with your members for the other 360 days of the year.

Approach
The Initiate Methodology

You know how to run an association, we know how to run conferences. Producing a two day conference typically takes up to nine months, with numerous different skillsets required at key times. We know that this isn’t your core area so at Initiate.Network we remove the upfront cost and risk from the association but still deliver revenue through a profit sharing arrangement. Why would we take all of the risk? We know what it takes to produce a top quality and successful conference so we don’t see any risk.

  • The Plan

    Understanding your needs

    Grow your membership base, represent your industry, enforce regulation, educate your members. There are many, often parallel, needs of an association. Whatever the goals, we work with you to develop a comprehensive project plan, demonstrating timelines and deliverables aligned to the your needs.

  • The Partnership

    Our expertise and resources join yours

    At Initiate.Network we understand that associations have limited budgets and need to be risk averse. The partnership is based around profit sharing. We cover the upfront production costs of the event and you provide marketing to your members and your wider audience. We will work together on the marketing approach to ensure that it reflects your brand and message.

  • Research

    Translating awareness into understanding

    The success of a conference is determined by the upfront research. After discussing the state of the market with you we perform primary and secondary research to understand the market challenges, explore untapped solutions to the challenges being faced and identify the leaders, innovators and disruptors, in the industry.

  • Production

    Delivering a world-class agenda

    With over 10 years of experience in conference production we can source and secure the most sought after leaders and innovators in the industry, which is vital to a high profile event. During production we also engineer the experience that we want our attendees to have which will tie in to your business objectives - more talks, more breakouts, larger gaps during the day.

  • Event & Sponsorship

    Strategic partnering to drive growth

    We offer a range of over 100 unique sponsorship opportunities from exhibition stands, stage branding, website advertising, branding on badges to intimate c-level dinners. Our sponsorship team work with you and the vendors to ensure optimum value for all parties.

  • Sales & Marketing

    Attract, convert, enhance and grow

    We believe in an ‘inbound’ sales and marketing approach. Through the production phase we will develop high quality and relevant, dual-branded content which we will publish through the associations internet sites, member lists and social channels. The marketing material will be developed with the aim of enhancing the reputation of both the association and the conference. We also invite and work with industry, national and international media to ensure that the event receives the maximum potential exposure. Our mission is to provide lasting benefit for our partner associations and we will not risk this goal for short term gains.

  • Event Management

    Excellent execution of an excellent strategy

    Because our team are involved in the process prior to, during and after the event, we ensure fluid execution and seamless integration with all conference objectives. We manage every last detail, from the running of the event: event planning, stage design, attendee registration, stage management, a/v to the management of sponsors on the day and the executing of private events.

  • On-going Communication

    Continue the momentum

    Our mission is to provide lasting benefit for our partner associations so we don’t just walk away after the conference. We produce content based on the event which can be delivered over an agreed schedule after the event to ensures that you stay front of mind with your members. Finally we will deliver a post-event analysis including attendee demographics, member/non member attendee ratio and more which can feed into your growth strategy for the coming year and to build on for next years event.
